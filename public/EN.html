About Kumarakapay

The name Kumarakapay comes from a Pemón camp located in the Gran Sabana. Kumarakapay was the camp that welcomed my family and me on our visit in 2016. So this chatbot is a thank you to them and the entire Pemon community.

My dream has always been to travel the world now it has become to know Venezuela, to know hidden landscapes that only adventurers know. And that's how Kumarakapay was invented, from my desire of showing the places that I get to know while I travel through Venezuela, as much as COVID allows me. Right now, Kuma only shows my top 11 places that I have already visited and others that I do not know yet but that I have always dreamed of going.

Kumarakapay is friendly, intelligent and highly motivated, don't hesitate to chat with her. Kuma is still in beta and learns new things every time you interact with her. Ask her anything, that what she doesn't know now, she will learn.
